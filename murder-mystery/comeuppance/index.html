I. A Comeuppance
A muffled bang wakes everyone up. Moments later, everyone is out of their rooms, anxious on what happened. The Groundskeeper goes upstairs to check up on the Aristocrat, but is not found in his room. After looking around, they find the body in the Servant's Quarters.

On the floor, just beside the body is a crumpled piece of note saying,

"Servant's quarters. Midnight."
"Come, or your old sins will come to you."
