Game Mechanics
I. Characters
Players choose among six characters, one of which is the murderer.
II. The Chapters
i. The Mini Puzzles
On each Chapter, players race to solve a mini puzzle to earn units of energy ( ) .

Chapters 1, 2, 3: 1st, 2nd, 3rd gets +3 energy
                                rest gets +2

Chapters 4, 5, 6: 1st gets +4
                                2nd, 3rd gets +3
                                rest gets +2

To investigate, you can ask a character about another character, or about themselves. Each investigation costs 1 .
Each round, players are required to investigate at least once.

ii. The Investigations
There are four types of character investigations:
1. Locked - available from Chapter IV onwards.
2. Alibi - reveals a character's alibi, Chapter V onwards.
3. Secret - reveals a character's secret, Chapter VI onwards.
4. Normal - available from Chapter I.

III. Accusations
Players will then accuse the character they think is guilty. An accusation can only be made on a character you have fully investigated on.

Ex. An accusation on Character X requires a player to ask every player about X, and this includes asking X about themselves.
