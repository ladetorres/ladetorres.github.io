VI. Swan Song
In the middle of the commotion, the murderer rushes back inside the Lodge, and locks the main door behind him.

They collect the fuel cans kept in the storage room and poured them all over the premises.

In one final deed, the murderer sets the liquid up, engulfing the Lodge in the fire. They stand there, full of content and finally at peace as they burn with the lodge, along with the secrets and the sins that are kept in it.
