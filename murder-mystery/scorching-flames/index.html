V. Scorching Flames
In the middle of the chaos, an explosion is heard, coming out from the gardens.

Everyone rushes out and sees the Forest Ranger's SUV aflame. There is a trail of slow burning fuse leading up to the vehicle. Someone must have sneaked out during all the investigation and lit the vehicle.

After a while, the fire is put out, but the SUV is beyond salvageable. Upon examination, inside it was the Magistrate's leather suitcase of old court files, and the Columnist's folder of write-ups related to the Mountain Lodge.
