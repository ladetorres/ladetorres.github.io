III. Old Sins
Even on close proximity, it takes great skill and familiarity to know how to fire the rifle.

On the wooden handle of the rifle is the name of the owner - the Travelling Missionary.

The rifle has been kept inside the Servant's Quarters for twelve years, and the room itself has been kept locked for that long.

Only the Aristocrat has the keys for that room, and the keys had never left his room, not until tonight.
